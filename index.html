How it Works

Doctors

Securely enter in children's health screening including height, weight, and age. This data is sent to local administrators and health organizations to track and improve food distribution.

Administrators

Track the nutrition and health status for children in a local community. With this information you can reach out to a sponsoring nonprofit to make sure your community's needs are being met.

Non Profits and Health Orgs

Using data sent from local administrators, better serve the needs of various communities by using data to more efficiently allocate food supplies to places in need.
